Miss Emily Lofland,  

First house south of Orphan's Home, Grand Avenue, near end of  
Lafayette Avenue,  
St. Louis, Mo.  
My compliments, and a Merry Christmas to you all.  

Jos. S. Roberson  

Send by messenger __early__ Christmas morning.  
All charges paid in San Francisco
